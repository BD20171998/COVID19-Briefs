COVID-19 Briefs

Comprehensive and current news center for the COVID-19 outbreak

Purpose

Welcome to COVID-19 Briefs, a website you can come to and see all your latest news regarding the COVID-19 Pandemic. Navigate through our website to browse news articles, Tweets, Reddit Posts, YouTube Videos, and view Statistics based on countries - all within the same website. Although there is not a shortage of content regarding the pandemic, it can be tough to keep tabs on all the different outlets to find news and discussions. On this site, there is not a goal to push "correct" information onto the user. COVID-19 Briefs hopes simply to bring all the things you want to see in the same place.
